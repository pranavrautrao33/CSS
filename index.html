The Legend of Cricket Mahi🏏❤️..!

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as Mahi, is a legendary Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains in the history of cricket. Under his leadership, India won numerous prestigious tournaments, including the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, and the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.
